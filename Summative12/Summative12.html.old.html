Assignment Overview

The purpose of this assignment is to learn the value of several special file formats that can be useful in getting things done.Some considerations are:

  • Combining several files into one to facilitate delivery by email, or during web conferencing and other collaborative endeavors
  • Work with .iso files to expedite distribution, simplify versioning and improve performance
  • Understand the file structure of .docx, .xlsx and pptx files
  • State the differences between the different styles of compression
  • Understand the importance of compression, and what sound frequency means.to different people (Mosquito?).

This assignment will cover two file formats-.iso and .zip. You are encouraged to explore on your own to find additional features and other file formats that will make your life and present or future jobs easier.
